About Atlantis

Atlantis offers fast and easy server hosting for Minetest, the open-source sandbox game inspired by Minecraft. Whether you're a player or an admin, our platform is designed to make your experience smooth and enjoyable.

  • Scalable Plans: Plans to fit all server sizes, from small groups to large communities.
