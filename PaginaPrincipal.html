About Atlantis

Atlantis provides high-quality server hosting specifically designed for Minetest, the open-source sandbox game inspired by Minecraft. Whether you're a casual player or a server admin, our hosting solutions are tailored to meet all your needs, from smooth performance to easy-to-use management tools.

Features

  • High Performance: Enjoy lag-free gameplay with optimized servers running on powerful hardware.
  • Easy Setup: Quick server setup with minimal effort – start your game world in minutes.
  • Custom Scripts: Use custom scripts and mods to personalize your server experience.
  • 24/7 Support: Our team is available around the clock to assist with any server-related issues.
  • Scalability: Host a server for a small group or a large community – our hosting plans scale with you.
